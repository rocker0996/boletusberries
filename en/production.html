Nature's bounty to your table

20,000 tons of selected wild mushrooms and berries

We are pleased to present our production ‚Äì this is not just processing of forest gifts, but a carefully built process that guarantees the highest quality and safety of each batch of products.

Our goal is to bring you all the benefits and unique taste of wild mushrooms and berries collected in environmentally clean regions, with compliance to the strictest European standards.

Scale and quality

Modern specialized lines

We have invested in advanced equipment specifically designed for gentle processing of wild mushrooms and berries. Our lines ensure maximum preservation of their natural properties, aroma and taste.

Each production stage begins with thorough raw material control. Experienced specialists conduct visual inspection, check freshness, absence of damage and foreign impurities. Only the best mushrooms and berries undergo further processing.

Strict quality selection

European standards

Our impressive volume is achieved through modern technologies and strict quality control. We comply with the strictest international quality and food safety standards.

Specialized equipment and multi-level control ensure maximum preservation of natural properties, aroma and taste of wild mushrooms and berries at every stage of production.

Shock freezing - freshness without losses

Shock freezing is a cooling technology by exposure to very low temperatures and cold air flow, which allows preserving the taste and nutritional properties of fresh products.

Unlike conventional freezing, where large ice crystals can form, this method prevents damage to the fruit structure.

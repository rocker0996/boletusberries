About our company

Boletus LT

LLC "Boletus LT" is a leading producer and supplier of wild mushrooms and berries in Russia. Our main activity is harvesting, processing, freezing, and selling wild mushrooms and berries.

Active activity of company owners in wild product procurement has been conducted since 2009. Over these years, we have accumulated vast experience and built a reliable network of procurement points located in many regions of Russia.

The company has its own production facilities with specialized processing lines, refrigeration units with modern "shock freezing" technology, and warehouse facilities located in Tyumen and Moscow regions.
